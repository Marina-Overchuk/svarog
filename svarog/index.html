About Us

Svarog Consulting is cost engineering and purchasing services company, established by a former executive from the automotive industry.

Our added value relies on talented people. Our team of consultants has a wide multicultural background of industrial projects and in-depth solid experience in both technical and economical fields.

We constantly strive for customer satisfaction by providing exceptional added value in our services.
